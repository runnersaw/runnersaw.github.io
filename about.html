SAWYER VAUGHAN
I have done a lot in my short life. I've been fascinated with science and technology since I was only a few feet off the ground. I used to love fixing cars with my dad in the driveway. I continued that passion through school with rigorous coursework that was mostly science-based.

But recently, I've found that what drives me most is creation and discovery.

I love spending afternoons curled up with my laptop working on my newest project. I've made implementations of artificial intelligences of the popular game 2048. I've tried to implement a web app to help users track and predict stocks. I've created a "robot" that follows a line using feedback loops. I made this website all on my own just because I could. I've made chess implementations and AIs, I've taken a stab at chatbots, and even modeled a basketball shot.

But I feel that all of that is still part of the story. Because that's what I've done, not who I am.

I am an Eagle Scout that abides by the Scout Law and Oath. I like helping others, I volunteer, and I do whatever I can for the community. I'm even finding this difficult, talking about myself. And hopefully all of this can give you just a slightly bigger glimpse at me.

Although, if you don't, I'm not offended.
